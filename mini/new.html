MIMA is the official entertainment association of St. Joseph's College (Autonomous), Bengaluru. Our students are known not just in the campus but in other colleges too. A talented and kind hearted pool of people make use of this association in creating social awareness on different realities and commercial themes through captivating Ice-breakers, Mad-Ads, Mock Rock and Mime. In the recent years MIMA has won several laurels and accolades keeping it as one of the best associations in SJC. Jesuit Education aims for holistic learning and greater knowing of the inner self. I believe MIMA is one association that brings the true potential of its students and helps them value themselves and the people around them.
