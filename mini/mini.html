Glimpse of MIMA


MIMA - Mad Ads, Ice Breakers, Mock Rock Association


MIMA is a comical theatre club of St. Joseph's College(Autonomous), Bangalore.
MIMA is one of the association of SJC with a tagline "Where everyone's mind goes crazy". The association has particpated in 60+ events in various colleges in the last 3 years. The one and only association in college with highest number of wins in an academic year(2019-2020) with 37. The association not only perform fun acts, but also perform acts like Mime, Streetplay to raise awareness among people. When it comes to the formation of the association, the work undergone to become an association is endless and inspiring.
